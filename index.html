FAQ

Common questions

Understanding your procedure and recovery process.

What is the consultation process?

You will meet directly with Dr. Kouli to discuss your goals. We use 3D imaging to visualize potential results and create a personalized surgical plan.

How long is recovery?

Recovery varies by procedure. Most non-surgical treatments have zero downtime, while surgeries typically require 1-2 weeks of rest.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We partner with CareCredit and Alphaeon Credit to offer flexible payment plans that fit your budget.

Is my consultation private?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict HIPAA guidelines. Your privacy and discretion are our top priorities from the moment you contact us.

Are you board certified?

Yes. Dr. Kouli is a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon with decades of experience in aesthetic and reconstructive surgery.

Where is the surgery performed?

All procedures are performed in our fully accredited, state-of-the-art private surgical facility for maximum safety and comfort.
